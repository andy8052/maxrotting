A Chrome extension that replaces everyday words with brainrot slang, transforming your browsing experience into pure unhinged internet culture.

## Installation

1. Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`
2. Enable **Developer mode** (toggle in top right corner)
3. Click **Load unpacked**
4. Select the `maxrotting` folder
5. Browse any website and witness the rot

## What This Extension Does

Maxrotting automatically replaces common English words with their brainrot equivalents as you browse the web. It uses a MutationObserver to handle dynamically loaded content (infinite scroll, SPAs, etc.), so the rot never stops.

## Glossary of Brainrot Terms

### The -Maxxing Suffix

**Origin:** Derived from "maximizing." Started in fitness/self-improvement communities, then spread to looksmaxxing forums.

**Meaning:** The act of optimizing or improving something to its maximum potential.

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Looksmaxxing** | Optimizing physical appearance through any means |
| **Gymmaxxing** | Maximizing gym/fitness efforts |
| **Brainmaxxing** | Maximizing intelligence/studying |
| **Sleepmaxxing** | Optimizing sleep for recovery/health |
| **Moneymaxxing** | Maximizing wealth accumulation |

### Cortisol Vocabulary (2025-2026 Meta)

**Origin:** Started as health/fitness discourse about the stress hormone, evolved into a looksmaxxing meme about stress affecting appearance, then became an ironic reaction format.

**The Meme:** "Low cortisol" = being unbothered, calm, superior. "Cortisol spike" = moment of stress, cringe, or shock. The joke is treating stress management as an aesthetic/status concern.

#### Core Terms

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Cortisol spike** | A moment of sudden stress, shock, or secondhand embarrassment |
| **Holy cortisol spike** | Exclamation for extreme stress/cringe moments |
| **Low cortisol** | State of being calm, unbothered, zen |
| **Cortisol face** | Puffy/bloated appearance allegedly caused by chronic stress |
| **Cortisol farming** | Deliberately causing stress to yourself or others |
| **"You're trying to spike my cortisol"** | Accusation that someone is deliberately stressing you out |

#### The Flex

Having "low cortisol" or being "cortisol-immune" is presented as the ultimate sigma state - nothing affects you, you're completely unbothered, your face stays chiseled because stress can't touch you.

## How It Works

The extension uses a content script that:

1. Builds a dictionary of word replacements
2. Walks through all text nodes in the DOM
3. Uses regex to find and replace whole words (preserving capitalization)
4. Observes DOM mutations to handle dynamically loaded content
5. Skips script, style, textarea, and input elements to avoid breaking functionality
